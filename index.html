About

A short introduction to who I am and what I do.

I am a multi-disciplinary Mechanical Engineer with over seven years of experience in building services design, specializing in plumbing, firefighting, and HVAC systems.

I have worked on high-profile healthcare, military, residential, commercial, and industrial projects in Kuwait, India, and Saudi Arabia, delivering functional, safe, and code-compliant systems.

My work combines strong engineering fundamentals with modern BIM tools such as Revit, Navisworks, BIM 360 and Dynamo. I enjoy building standards, automations, and reusable tools that make design work faster, more reliable, and easier to coordinate across disciplines.

Beyond design, I lead coordination with architects and structural engineers, support site teams, and guide junior engineers and technicians in adopting best practices.
